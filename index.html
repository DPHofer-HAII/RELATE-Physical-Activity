RELATE: Personalised AI Support for Physical Activity

We are investigating how AI-generated motivational messages can be personalised to support physical activity over four weeks.

What is the study about?

RELATE is a four-week research study exploring how personalised AI-generated messages can support your physical activity goals. The study consists of four parts:

  • Part 1 — Pre-study questionnaire (Day 1): Attitudes toward physical activity, trust in AI, current activity level, demographics, and your physical activity schedule for the first week — including which days you plan to be active and when you're open to receiving a motivational message (~20–25 minutes)
  • Part 2 — Intervention phase (Days 2–28): On the days you indicated you want to be physically active, you will receive a motivational message within the time window you defined. Before the message, a few brief questions; after the message, a short assessment (~2–3 minutes per session). At the end of each day where you scheduled activity, a very short questionnaire asks whether you did the activity you intended — or something else. Every 7 days, you update your activity schedule for the coming week and fill out a brief questionnaire on your physical activity attitudes (~5 minutes)
  • Part 3 — Post-study questionnaire (Day 29): Your overall experience with the system and any changes in your perception of AI-generated messages (~10–15 minutes). Requires a minimum of 12 successfully completed days to be eligible.
  • Optional Part 4 — Exit interview: A video call interview about your experience (limited places, first-come first-served, ~1 hour). Requires completion of Parts 1–3.

The total time commitment is approximately 3.5 hours (or 4.5 hours with the optional interview) spread across 30 days.

About the Messages

The motivational messages you receive are generated by a Large Language Model (LLM) — an AI system — based on contextual information you provide before each session (mood, stress, motivation, perceived barriers) and information collected at onboarding. Messages are intended as motivational prompts, not professional advice. As with any AI-generated content, outputs may occasionally be imprecise or not fully aligned with your situation. You are encouraged to use your own judgement.

Compensation

Compensation is based on Austrian minimum wage (€13.50/hour) and is tied to the number of days you successfully complete. A "successful day" means completing both the intervention questionnaire (with the motivational message) and the end-of-day follow-up on a day you scheduled physical activity.

You must complete a minimum of 12 successful days to be eligible for compensation. If you do not reach 12 days, you will not receive compensation and will not be eligible for the post-study questionnaire or exit interview.

Withdrawal & Rights

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. Please note that all research data is collected in anonymised form. Under GDPR, anonymised data used for scientific research purposes is not subject to the right to erasure. By signing up, you confirm that you are 18 or older, are fluent in English, and understand how your data will be used (see Data Protection section below).

Data Protection & Security

Information about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data We Collect

  • Pre-study questionnaire responses (attitudes toward physical activity, trust in AI, current activity level, demographics)
  • Weekly physical activity schedule (which days you plan to be active, preferred message time windows)
  • Contextual input before each message (mood, stress, self-efficacy, motivation, perceived barriers to PA)
  • Post-message assessments (perceived empathy, relevance, helpfulness, affective response)
  • End-of-day check-ins (whether you completed your planned activity)
  • Weekly follow-up questionnaires (attitudes toward physical activity)
  • Post-study questionnaire (overall system experience, Trust in AI)
  • Optional exit interview recordings and transcripts (for selected participants only)
  • Email address for study communication and compensation delivery

How We Use Your Data

  • Generate personalised motivational messages using an AI language model based on your questionnaire responses and contextual input
  • Conduct research analysis for scientific publications and conference presentations
  • Improve understanding of personalisation in digital health interventions
  • All published results will be anonymised — no identifying information will be disclosed

Data Storage & Security

  • All data stored on a local, institution-managed server at the Ludwig Boltzmann Institute
  • Data is pseudonymised using unique participant IDs — personal identifiers kept separate from research data
  • The ID-to-name mapping document (required for reimbursement) is accessible only to the principal investigator and permanently deleted after compensation is disbursed
  • Access limited to authorised research team members only
  • Data retained for 10 years per Austrian research data retention guidelines, then securely deleted

Your Rights as a Participant

  • Participation is completely voluntary
  • Right to withdraw from active participation at any time without penalty
  • All research data is collected in anonymised form using participant IDs with no link to your identity
  • Under GDPR Article 17(3)(d), anonymised data processed for scientific research purposes is exempt from the right to erasure
  • Right to file complaints with the Austrian Data Protection Authority
